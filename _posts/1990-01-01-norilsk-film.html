Norilsk Film

Norisk Film is an interactive web-documentary film about Norilsk, the Northern city of Russia, in interactive mode.

Production:
2015, ONY agency. Video in case by Artem Zamashnoy
Role:
Use case, UX prototyping, user interface, visual design
Links:
Awwwards SOTD, Red Dot Award, KMFR (Gold), ADCR (Gold), White Square (Silver)
+ Credits

3D video-panoramas and a historical timeline will guide you through the stories of people who used to live in the city with temperature below -50°C.
